About Us

Who We Are

We are specialists in organisational learning, academic quality, workforce development, and performance improvement. Led by a Senior Fellow of Advance HE, Learning and Teaching Lead, strategist and internationally experienced consultant, we bring over 25 years of experience in education, leadership, and professional practice, alongside more than 15 years of experience in private-sector leadership, business development and strategic coaching.

Our expertise spans educational leadership, organisational strategy, learning and development, teaching excellence, quality assurance and capability building.

We support higher education institutions, private organisations, and public sector bodies to strengthen performance, develop people, and achieve sustainable growth.

Our Focus

Our work focuses on two complementary areas: Higher Education Consultancy and Learning and Workforce Development.

In higher education, we support curriculum design, teaching and learning enhancement, quality assurance, employability, widening participation, and student success.

In organisations, we help leaders strengthen workforce capability through learning and development, leadership training, organisational learning, performance improvement, and strategic capability development.

Our solutions are designed to improve outcomes, strengthen quality, enhance performance, and support long-term organisational success.

Higher Education Consultancy

Curriculum design, teaching and learning enhancement, quality assurance, employability, widening participation, and student success.

Learning & Workforce Development

Leadership training, organisational learning, performance improvement, and strategic capability development for organisations.
